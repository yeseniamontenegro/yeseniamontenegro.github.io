About



Hi, I'm Yesenia!


I'm a sophomore at the University of Maryland, double majoring in journalism and Germanic studies. I'm originally from Germany, but I've lived in Maryland for the past nine years. I first got involved in journalism in high school, when I joined the school newspaper during my freshman year. I started out as a reporter and then worked my way up to the editor-in-chief during my senior year. I loved writing articles and editing them as well. I decided to combine Germanic studies with my journalism major so I will hopefully have the oportunity to move back to Germany at some point, or work for a German news organization in the U.S. I would love to work as a foreign correspondent and combine my German heritage with my American one.

Currently, I'm a reporter for Her Campus at Maryland, and serve as the assistant marketing manager. I'm also a staff writer for a publication called the COVID-19 Observer. This semester I am working as an editorial intern at Baltimore magazine.

Feel free to look through my portfolio to learn more about my experience in journalism and to read through some of my published work!
