Hi, I'm Yesenia!

I'm a sophomore at the University of Maryland, double majoring in journalism and Germanic studies. I'm originally from Germany, but I've lived in Maryland for the past nine years. My interest in journalism started in high school, when I joined the school newspaper during my freshman year. I started out as a reporter and then worked as the editor-in-chief during my senior year. I loved writing articles and editing them as well. I decided to combine Germanic studies with my journalism major so I will hopefully have the oportunity to move back to Germany at some point, or work for a German news organization in the U.S.

Currently, I'm a reporter for Her Campus at Maryland, and serve as the assistant marketing manager. I also write for a publication called the COVID-19 Observer. I am currently an editorial intern at Baltimore magazine.
